Who We Are

A Trusted Name in Trading & Contracting Since 2010

Founded in 2010, Sharooq Al-Fajer (Al-Arabiya Trading Company) has grown into a multi-disciplinary company serving clients across the Sultanate of Oman, the wider Gulf region, and China. From heavy civil works and building construction to international trade, logistics, and event management, we bring reliability, compliance, and hands-on expertise to every project we undertake.

Our strength lies in combining five specialised divisions under one roof, giving our clients a single, accountable partner for complex, end-to-end requirements.
